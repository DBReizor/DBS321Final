What is AI?

AI (Artificial Intelligence) is a technology that enables computers and other various devices to perform advanced functions, including understanding our languages and responding to our questions/thoughts. AI has been around for a while now, integrating into a lot of different aspects of our lives.

The most prominent AI tool we all know is ChatGPT, which has been around since 2022. Whether it's for helping advance software, problem-solving, or enhancing learning, it's been a helpful tool for all sorts of technology. While it has these advantages, it also has its disadvantages.
